## Key Findings

**Business:** CRDO designs high-speed connectivity semiconductors (SerDes, optical DSPs, line cards) that solve the bandwidth bottleneck inside and between AI data centers. Revenue is concentrated in hyperscalers — a risk that is also a moat signal.

**Technology moat:** Custom silicon co-designed with hyperscaler engineering teams creates 2–3 year switching cost cycles. Not easily displaced by merchant silicon because the performance requirements exceed what general-purpose chips can deliver.

**Alt data signals:** Hiring data showed accelerating headcount growth in AI-specific applications engineering roles — a leading indicator of pipeline expansion ahead of formal announcements.

**Market:** Global data center interconnect TAM is projected to grow from ~$6B (2024) to $20B+ (2028), driven almost entirely by AI training and inference cluster buildout.
