# Stage 02 — AI Infrastructure Stock Universe

**Perplexity Task:** Task 2  
**Purpose:** Map the full investment universe of AI infrastructure stocks across risk/reward tiers

---

## What This Stage Produced

A comprehensive, structured universe of publicly listed companies (NYSE/NASDAQ, market cap > $1B) solving the six structural flaws of current AI systems. Stocks are organized into five risk/reward tiers to give the pitch intellectual rigor — we are not cherry-picking CRDO; we are selecting it from a defensible, exhaustive universe.

### The Six AI Flaw Categories Researched

| Category | Structural Problem Solved |
|---|---|
| Synthetic data generation | Training data quality, bias, scarcity |
| AI data curation & labeling | High-quality annotation at scale |
| Simulation environments | Agent training, robotics, autonomous systems |
| Inference optimization | Cost per query, energy efficiency |
| AI memory & context | Statelessness, context window limits |
| Human behavioral data | Embodied AI, robotics, preference learning |

## Perplexity Capabilities Used

- **7 parallel sub-agents** — one per AI flaw category plus one for cross-sector analysis:
  - Each sub-agent independently researched its category, identifying listed companies, market caps, moat characteristics, and early financial signals
  - Results were synthesized into a unified risk/reward tier matrix
- **Premium sources:** CB Insights (private/public company intelligence), PitchBook (comparable deals and market sizing)

---

## The Five Risk/Reward Tiers

| Tier | Label | Profile | Stage 3 Winner | Composite Score |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Conservative Anchor | Proven platform, premium multiple, low volatility | NVDA | 8.80 |
| 2 | Established Grower | High growth, defensible moat, execution premium | PLTR | 7.75 |
| 3 | High Conviction Growth | Mispriced, moat-gated, non-consensus | **CRDO** | **8.55** |
| 4 | Speculative Asymmetric | Catalyst-dependent, binary re-rating potential | ZETA | 7.50 |
| 5 | Deep Value / Turnaround | FCF machine, compliance moat, activist-ripe | BOX | 6.00 |

CRDO was selected from Tier 3 — High Conviction Growth, with a composite score of 8.55/10. It scored highest of any finalist on non-consensus advantage (9/10) and data advantage (9/10) — the dimensions most relevant to the competition's 30pt Perplexity criterion. NVDA had the highest overall score (8.80) but was rejected: every judge already owns it and knows the thesis, making it the wrong pitch for this audience.
